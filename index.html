Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Across the 2nd Dimension

Phineas and Ferb movie is about family channels favourite tv show ‘Phineas and Ferb’ where the boys and their sister are sucked into another dimension where they meet the total opposites of themselves. In this dimension they also discover their pet platypus perry is a secret agent working to stop Dr. Doofenshmirtz, who is the creator of the machine that sent the group to the second dimension.

In the second Dimension, Dr. Doofenshmirtz has already taken over the tri state area and the whole city is taken over by him and his bots. Once the boys discover the truth about their pet they have to work with him to stop the evil as Dr. Doofenshmirtz tries to enter other dimensions to take them over also. The Candace in this dimension is also a warrior agents who is trying to stop her dimensions Dr. Doofenshmirtz and works with her dimension brother and her brother from the other dimension to put an end to the multiple dimension open.
